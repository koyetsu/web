About BlueWave Print Studio

Founded in 1998 by lifelong Corpus Christi residents Ana and Mateo Castillo, BlueWave Print Studio started as a small-format print shop helping neighborhood schools and non-profits. Today, our 12-person team pairs design-forward thinking with state-of-the-art production technology to deliver consistent, color-accurate results for businesses across South Texas.

Community roots

We reinvest 5% of annual profits into local art programs, youth sports, and beach cleanups.

Certified sustainability

BlueWave is FSC®-certified and offers recycled, post-consumer paper stocks for every product line.

Partners in growth

From branding refreshes to multi-location signage rollouts, we serve as a dedicated print partner for 200+ area businesses.
